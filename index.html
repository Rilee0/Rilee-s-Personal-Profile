About Me

My name is Rilee Young and I am a year 10 at Wakatipu High School. I was born in Auckland, and moved down to Queenstown in April 2018. I live in Shotover country with my mum, dad and sister. I went to Shotover Primary School until the end of year 6, before moving to Queenstown Primary School. I have been attending Wakatipu High for one and a half years. I enjoy exploring the backcountry on foot and bike with my family and friends.
